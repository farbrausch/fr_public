Twirl Operator

 

The Twirl Operator applies a spiral shaped distortion to a texture.

 

Inputs

 

One texture.

 

Parameters

 

Strength

Controls the effect amplification.

 

Gamma

Controls the spiral loop growth factor.

 

Radius (X, Y)

Sets the overall spiral size.

 

Center (X, Y)

Sets the spiral position.

 

Border clamp

off/x/y/both: Off: border pixels wrap. On: border pixels clamp.
